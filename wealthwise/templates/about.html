Who Are We

Wealthwise.com is a financial service firm with a focus on digital assets. Wealthwise.com team combines deep technical expertise in the emerging asset class with decades of experience launching investment vehicles for a wide variety of market participants. The firm is led by experienced financial veterans with decades of experience across firms including Mabcredit, Lowry Consulting GMBH, Guggenheim Partners, and Chicago Board of Trade. Wealthwise.com has deep expertise in traditional asset management with extensive background in Fund management, Portfolio management, Investment Banking, and scaling financial products with major financial firms. We aim to be the expert across the universe of investment and digital assets.
